About TUNU Publishers

"Elimu ni Tunu" — celebrating educational excellence, cultural heritage, and critical thinking with curriculum-centered learning in Kenya.

Who We Are

Established in Kenya with a vision to revolutionize learning materials, TUNU Publishers has grown to become a reliable, household publishing name across the country. The Swahili word "Tunu" translates to a rare gift, treasure, or gem — we operate on the profound philosophy that "Elimu ni Tunu" (Education is a Treasure).

Our mission centers on producing exceptionally curated materials aligned with the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC). From Junior Secondary revisions to culturally resonant stories, our materials ensure concepts are absorbed with clarity, critical thinking, and joy.

Whether you're a parent looking for imaginative grade-appropriate storybooks like Kosa la Popo, or a secondary student navigating critical studies with reference titles like Kudurusu Fasihi, we walk alongside you at every stage of the academic journey.

Our Vision

To be a leading partner in the literary journey of authors, empowering them to bring their stories to life. With excellence and creativity, we envision a world where every voice is heard and every book reaches its full potential, inspiring readers globally.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive publishing solution that covers every aspect of the book creation process — from curriculum-aligned content development to distribution — for learners and institutions across Kenya.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We work with experienced curriculum panels, language specialists, and designers to produce flawless, reliable, and aesthetically pleasing books.

Cultural Relevance

We believe learning resonates best when it mirrors local experiences. Our books elevate local storylines and values suited for the African learner.

Innovation

We constantly refine our layout styles, revision metrics, and illustrations to meet the modern educational requirements of the 21st-century learner.

Integrity

Working closely with regulatory bodies (such as KICD), teachers, and educational institutions, we uphold the highest ethical standards in our text production.

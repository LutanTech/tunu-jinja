Delivery Policy

Everything you need to know about how, when and where we deliver your books across Kenya.

Processing Time

1 – 2 business days

Nairobi Delivery

Same day – 2 business days

Countrywide

2 – 5 business days

1. Order Processing

Once your order is placed and payment is confirmed via M-Pesa, our team packages and dispatches your books within 1–2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next working day. You will receive an SMS or email confirmation once your order has been dispatched.

2. Delivery Zones & Timeframes

We deliver to all 47 counties in Kenya through trusted courier partners. Estimated delivery timeframes after dispatch are as follows:

Zone Estimated Delivery
Nairobi & EnvironsSame day – 2 business days
Major Towns (Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, etc.)2 – 3 business days
Other Counties & Rural Areas3 – 5 business days

3. Delivery Fees

Delivery fees are calculated based on your location and parcel weight and will be confirmed by our team before dispatch. We work with courier and rider partners to keep delivery costs as low as possible and we occasionally run free-delivery promotions for orders above a set value — watch our homepage and newsletter for updates.

4. Order Tracking

Once dispatched, you'll receive tracking details via SMS or email where applicable. You can also reach out to us directly on WhatsApp or by phone with your order ID for a status update at any time.

5. Failed or Delayed Deliveries

If a delivery attempt fails because you were unavailable or the address provided was incomplete or incorrect, our courier partner will attempt to contact you to reschedule. Please ensure your phone number and delivery address are accurate at checkout — additional delivery attempts may attract extra courier fees.

6. Damaged or Incorrect Items

We carefully inspect and package every order before dispatch. If you receive a damaged, defective or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with your order ID and a photo of the item and we'll arrange a replacement or refund at no extra cost to you.

7. Institutional & Bulk Orders

Schools, libraries and institutions ordering in bulk can request custom delivery arrangements, including scheduled deliveries across a term or academic year. Reach out to our sales team to discuss bulk delivery terms.

8. Questions About Your Delivery

For any delivery-related questions, reach us at support@tunupublishers.com, call +254 722 68 21 29 or message us on WhatsApp. We're here Monday – Saturday, 8:00am – 6:00pm.

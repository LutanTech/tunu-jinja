About TUNU Publishers

Home / About Us

Nurturing African Excellence

TUNU Publishers is a leading educational publishing house in Kenya, dedicated to producing high-quality, innovative, and culturally relevant learning materials for the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC).

Since our inception, we have been at the forefront of the educational transformation in East Africa, working closely with educators, curriculum developers, and creative talents to ensure that every learner has access to tools that spark curiosity and foster a lifelong love for learning.

Our books are crafted not just to meet examination requirements, but to build character, competence, and confidence in the African child.

Our Vision

To be the premier publisher of choice in Africa, recognized for excellence, innovation, and the empowerment of every learner through transformative knowledge.

Our Mission

To produce globally competitive educational resources that are deeply rooted in African values, accessible to all, and supportive of the dynamic needs of modern education.

Our Values

Integrity in content, commitment to quality, inclusivity in representation, and a passion for the educational growth of the African continent.
